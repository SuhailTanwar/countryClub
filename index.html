About Us

When Country Club Hospitality and Holidays Ltd. was established in 1989, it was a pioneer in the conception of offering comprehensive leisure, recreation and hospitality membership services in India. Constituted as a fully equipped club, Country Club has now flourished into a provider of member exclusive clubbing hubs, breathtaking holiday destinations, avant-garde fitness centres and star-studded events.

The Country Club is the proud owner of over 50 properties in India, the Middle East, Thailand and Sri Lanka with the offering of over 4000 Country Vacations global associate properties. This colossal network encompasses social clubs in urban hotspots, luxurious city-centric hotels, rejuvenating wellness centres, beach-front resorts, serene hilltop vacation homes, wildlife jungle lodges and water amusement parks.

Popularly termed as the Powerhouse of Entertainment, the Country Club finds a reason to celebrate every day. From religious festivals to special occasions, every global holiday is treated with utmost importance. Our team of creative minds get together to plan extravagant events with celebrity performances exclusively for our members. The Country Club also periodically organises family amusement events including Live Musical Nights, Food Festivals, Magic Shows and Tambola.

To provide round-the-clock seamless member services, the Country Club offers an exclusive Mobile App and a web portal to expedite real-time holiday reservation, payment transfer, customer service and membership updating facilities.

But that is not all. At Country Club we put the environment and our society at the forefront of everything we do. Hence, the Country Club supports several key CSR initiatives including schooling of under-privileged children, environmental sustainability projects, community health aid and social well-being missions. This is what connects us to the community whilst keeping us grounded.

Company Statement

Our Mission

It is our mission to effectuate exclusive, world-class leisure experiences to our cherished members in a relaxing, eco-friendly environment. We aim to endorse a healthy, robust lifestyle with our recreational facilities. We work to develop long term member relationships by constantly staying with the times and creating state-of-the-art spaces. In doing so we also keep in mind the environment around us, hence ethically merging with the ecosystem.

Our Vision

Already a pioneer in multiple capacities, it is our vision to emerge as a wold leader in the leisure hospitality industry in terms of number of properties and size of our member base. We aim to successfully surpass the benchmarks of the clubbing, holiday, fitness, leisure and entertainment industries, while further diversifying into related business segments. All this to be able to give our members an unmatched experience.
